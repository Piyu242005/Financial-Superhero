Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What is Finology?

Finology is a comprehensive fintech platform that helps you make smarter financial decisions. We offer AI-powered financial advice, calculators, and educational resources to help you grow your wealth.

How does the AI Assistant work?

Our AI Assistant uses RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) technology to provide accurate answers to your financial questions. It's trained on comprehensive financial knowledge including Indian tax laws, investment strategies, and market basics.

Is Finology free to use?

Yes! Basic features including calculators, AI chat, and learning resources are completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future with advanced portfolio tracking and personalized recommendations.

How do I start investing?

Start by opening a Demat account with any broker like Zerodha or Groww. Build an emergency fund first, then begin with SIPs in index funds. Our AI Assistant can guide you through the entire process!
